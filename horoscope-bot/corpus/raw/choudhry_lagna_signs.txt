The sign Leo is ruled by the planet, the Sun, signifying
intelligence, heart, male progeny, royality and magnificence.
This sign is fiery, positive, barren and signifies benevolence.
Leo born people with weak and/or afflicted Sun in the chart face
weak health, weak digestion, malfunctioning of heart, disease
of spine and blood, lack of stamina and strong will power and
weak eyesight.
Leos are rulers, extrovert, best organisers, patient listeners,
jealous, decisive, art lovers, generous, authoritative and
anxious to retain and expand their domain. As the weakness
prone planet, Mercury, rules their second house ruling kingdom,

third house.

PISCES ASCENDANT
The sign Pisces is ruled by Jupiter and rules feet, toes,
lymphatic system, bones of feet and toe, etc. Afflicted and
. weak Jupiter together with close affliction of functional malefics
to the midpoint of the ascendant gives gout pains, joint pains,
problems of blood circulation and the diseases connected with
the parts ruled by this sign.
Pisces is a negative, non-human, watery and fruitful sign
and signifies enjoyments, sensitiveness, etc. Mercury gets
debilitated in this sign and Venus gets exalted in this sign. With
strong and well placed Jupiter in the chart, it gives a
spectacular rise in the career.
Pisceans are restless and are always on the move. They
are generally devoted to duty, have strong moral value and are
good advisors whenever Jupiter and/or Mercury are strong
in the horoscope. They are idealistic, generous, emotional
and enthusiastic.
As Jupiter rules the tenth house and Mars rules the second
house the Pisceans enjoy good status in life in the professions
of administration, training and development. They are best
suited for advisory roles in the fields of finance, commerce and
law. The sign Cancer in their fifth house makes them highly
sensitive and emotional. The weak and afflicted placement of
the Moon gives emotional set backs in life. The weakness
prone planet Mercury makes the marital life, of Pisceans
unscuccessful. A strong Sun in the nativity without afflicting
to other natal positions blesses the native with good health
while a similarly placed Saturn gives long life and Venus gives
a long lease of marital life. However, transit close afflictions
of the Sun, Saturn and Venus do trigger the inauspicious
significant events quite oftenly in life,
The planets Jupiter, Mars, Mercury and the Moon are
functional benefics for this ascendant. The presence of these
planets in strength in a nativity assures a happy married life, a
successful career, happiness on account of progeny and a good
status in life. On the other hand the planets the Sun, Saturn
